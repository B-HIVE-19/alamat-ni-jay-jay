## Assignment Rule

Do not assign a reserved ID to a final deliverable until the owner, purpose, linked document, status, and dependencies are known.

## Retirement Rule

Retired IDs should remain in this registry with a Deprecated or Archived status. They should not be reused for unrelated work.
