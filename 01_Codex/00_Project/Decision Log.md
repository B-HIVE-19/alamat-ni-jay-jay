## Purpose

The Decision Log is the official historical record of approved creative and production decisions for *Alamat ni Jay Jay*.

It preserves how Alamat evolved over time. Existing decisions must never be overwritten or erased. If a decision changes, the original decision remains in the log and the revision is recorded as a new decision or version entry.

## Decision Entry Requirements

Every approved creative decision must include:

- Decision Number.
- Title.
- Date.
- Status.
- Reason.
- Discussion Summary.
- Impact on Future Development.
- Dependencies.
- Canon Status.
- Version History.

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Approved | Active project decision. |
| Revised | Superseded or updated by a later decision, preserved for history. |
| Deprecated | No longer active, preserved for historical context. |
| Pending Review | Proposed but not yet approved. |
