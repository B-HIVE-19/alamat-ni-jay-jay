## Purpose

The Alamat Codex is the authoritative design and production knowledge base for *Alamat ni Jay Jay*.

Its purpose is to preserve creative intent, maintain continuity, protect canon, and provide a single source of truth for everyone contributing to the project.

The Codex exists so that the heart of the game remains consistent regardless of how long development takes or how many contributors join the project.

## Mission

The Alamat Codex protects the identity of *Alamat ni Jay Jay*.

It records not only what decisions were made, but also why they were made, ensuring that future development remains faithful to the original vision.

## Guiding Principles

- Canon is intentional.
- Documentation precedes implementation whenever practical.
- Characters are people before they are gameplay roles.
- Adventure is the foundation of every design decision.
- Wonder takes precedence over spectacle.
- Filipino culture inspires the world while remaining completely original intellectual property.
- Preserve emotional authenticity above technical complexity.

## Canon Authority

Creative canon is determined solely by the Creative Director.

No Codex Entry becomes official canon until approved.

Suggestions, drafts, brainstorming sessions, and exploratory ideas are not considered canon until formally approved.

## Document Lifecycle

Every Codex Entry follows the same lifecycle.

1. Draft
2. Internal Review
3. Revision
4. Creative Director Approval
5. Canon
6. Archived, if superseded

Superseded entries are never deleted.

The evolution of the project is part of its history and should always remain preserved.

## Canon Rules

Canon decisions may only be changed through an approved revision.

Retcons should be extremely rare.

Whenever possible, new ideas should expand existing lore rather than overwrite established canon.

The Alamat Codex exists to preserve consistency throughout the lifetime of the project.

## Documentation Philosophy

Every Codex Entry should answer four questions.

1. What is this?
2. Why does it exist?
3. How does it support the vision?
4. What should future developers never accidentally change?

Documentation should preserve not only information but also creative intent.

## Studio Philosophy

*Alamat ni Jay Jay* is not built to chase industry trends.

It is built to create a timeless adventure centered on friendship, exploration, humility, curiosity, and hope.

Every contributor should ask one question before proposing any creative change:

"Does this make the world of Alamat stronger?"

If the answer is no, the proposal should be reconsidered.
