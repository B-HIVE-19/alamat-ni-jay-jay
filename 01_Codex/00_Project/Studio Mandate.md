## Purpose

This mandate defines how 1910 Creative Studios approaches creative and production decisions for *Alamat ni Jay Jay*.

The team is not here to generate random ideas. The team is here to function like a professional game development studio building an original commercial-quality JRPG.

## Studio Role

Every contribution should be evaluated through the lens of experienced RPG development:

- Creative direction.
- Narrative design.
- Worldbuilding.
- Character development.
- Dungeon design.
- Quest design.
- Gameplay systems.
- Combat design.
- UI and player experience.
- Concept art and visual identity.
- Music and audio direction.
- Technical architecture.
- Production continuity.

## Canon Protection

Previously established canon must be preserved.

Approved lore, approved design direction, and approved emotional targets may not be overwritten without explicit permission from Jeffrey Moralde.

When a new idea conflicts with established canon, the conflict must be identified before expansion.

## Creative Evaluation Standard

New ideas should not be expanded simply because they are interesting. They must be evaluated against:

1. The approved Vision.
2. The approved Design Pillars.
3. The north-star test of wonder, curiosity, and adventure.
4. Filipino-inspired authenticity without stereotype.
5. Character, world, and dungeon consistency.
6. Emotional authenticity.
7. Long-term project identity.

## Primary Inspiration Boundary

The primary inspiration is the feeling of adventure found in classic JRPGs such as the original *Phantasy Star*.

The project does not copy *Phantasy Star* story, characters, world, maps, factions, terminology, or specific content.

The goal is to recreate the emotional experience of:

- Embarking on a grand adventure.
- Exploring mysterious worlds.
- Entering dangerous first-person dungeons.
- Discovering meaningful secrets.
- Traveling with memorable companions.
- Feeling wonder, risk, and momentum.

## Original Identity

*Alamat ni Jay Jay* is a completely original intellectual property inspired by Filipino folklore, landscapes, architecture, traditions, values, island life, and ancient-civilization mystery.

The work must protect the identity of Alamat.

## Non-Negotiable Protections

Protect consistency.

Protect emotional authenticity.

Protect cultural specificity.

Protect player wonder.

Protect the feeling of adventure.

Protect characters as people.

Protect dungeons as meaningful places.

Protect Alamat as its own world.
