## Purpose

The Development Roadmap organizes the long-term production path for *Alamat ni Jay Jay* without locking story spoilers, final scope, staffing, budget, release dates, or platform commitments.

The roadmap should be revised as the project matures, but it must always protect the Studio Vision, Project Charter, Alamat Doctrine, Studio Standards, and Decision Log.

## Now

Pre-production foundation.

- Maintain the Alamat Codex as the single source of truth.
- Preserve approved canon and document new decisions.
- Establish the official bibles for vision, doctrine, story, characters, world, creatures, dungeons, art, music, systems, UI, technical architecture, and quests.
- Develop Jay Jay, Kalika, and the earliest companion relationships with emotional authenticity.
- Define the first playable adventure loop: village, overworld travel, first-person dungeon, turn-based combat, discovery reward, and return-to-safety rhythm.
- Create prototype standards for first-person dungeon exploration inspired by the emotional experience of classic JRPG adventure.
- Begin creature, dungeon, and region concepts only when they support wonder, curiosity, and the tribute heart of the project.

## Next

Vertical slice planning.

- Select the first region, first village, and first dungeon candidate for production exploration.
- Build a small playable proof of exploration, combat, navigation, dialogue, and reward flow.
- Define initial art direction targets for character sprites, dungeon perspective, UI readability, and environmental identity.
- Define initial music direction targets for opening mood, village warmth, dungeon danger, battle energy, and emotional motifs.
- Create production templates for quests, encounters, items, enemies, NPCs, and dungeon maps.
- Test whether the first playable sequence makes 12-year-old Jeff excited to keep playing.

## Later

Full production planning.

- Expand the world map, party roster, dungeon roster, story acts, combat systems, equipment, magic, quests, and production pipeline.
- Create milestone plans for prototype, vertical slice, alpha, beta, release candidate, launch, and archival preservation.
- Build QA, localization, accessibility, save-system, performance, and content-review processes.
- Preserve the emotional reason the project exists through every scope decision.
