## Transportation Design Intent

Travel should be one of the world’s defining experiences. Routes should create adventure, danger, social encounters, and discovery.

## Travel Modes

| Mode | Use | Story and Gameplay Value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Outrigger Boat | Coastal and island travel | Route choice, storms, reefs, port access. |
| River Boat | Interior travel | Village connections, ambushes, shrine crossings. |
| Ferry | Controlled crossings | Toll disputes, NPC encounters, political access. |
| Footpath | Local travel | Exploration, hidden shrines, creature encounters. |
| Mountain Trail | Highland access | Resource tension, danger, isolation. |
| Rope Bridge | Gorge and river traversal | Fear, maintenance, local control. |
| Ancient Causeway | Ruin-linked travel | Tidal puzzles, shortcuts, lost history. |
| Mangrove Channel | Hidden travel | Navigation challenge, secrecy, ecology. |

## Route Design Rules

- Routes should have owners, risks, stories, and maintenance problems.
- Travel should change by season, weather, reputation, and political tension.
- Shortcuts should feel earned through exploration, trust, or ancient knowledge.
