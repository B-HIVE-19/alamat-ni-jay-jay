## Economy Design Intent

The economy should make the islands feel connected. Items, rumors, weapons, songs, recipes, and conflicts should travel along the same routes as food and trade goods.

WRLD-001 establishes Vyra as the protected canonical national currency. Alamat operates as a modern fantasy society where citizens use Vyra through physical currency, banking institutions, digital payments, salaries, military payroll, commercial trade, taxation, and everyday purchases.

Vyra should feel familiar to modern players while remaining grounded in Alamat's national history, Amethyst symbolism, and the Chikagoland Amethyst Crown.

WRLD-002 establishes the House of Lexi as the protected merchant hospitality standard. Shops should not feel like anonymous transaction points; they should be places of welcome, trust, practical care, story exchange, and memory.

## Trade Pressures

- Storm seasons raise prices and isolate islands.
- Dangerous reefs make navigators politically important.
- Closed shrines or cursed routes can damage local economies.
- Ruins create illegal markets for relics.
- Merchant houses may control debt more than land.

House of Lexi spaces should make travelers feel recognized. A purchase may include a rumor, joke, warning, kindness, small gift, local story, or relationship beat.
