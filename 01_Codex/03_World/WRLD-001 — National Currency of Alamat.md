## Purpose

Establish the official national currency of Alamat and its relationship to the Chikagoland Amethyst Crown, modern commerce, banking, trade, taxation, military pay, UI, inventory, and gameplay systems.

## Official Currency

The official currency of the nation is:

Vyra.

Vyra is used for all commerce, banking, salaries, military pay, trade, taxation, and daily transactions throughout the world of Alamat.

The word "Vyra" functions as both singular and plural.

Examples:

- 1 Vyra.
- 25 Vyra.
- 350 Vyra.
- 12,500 Vyra.

## Origin

The origins of Vyra trace back to the Chikagoland Amethyst Crown.

Following the Great Fire of Chikagoland, the Crown became the enduring symbol of hope, resilience, unity, and compassionate leadership.

When the nation rebuilt, its first official currency was minted under the authority of the Crown.

Although the economy has evolved through centuries of commerce, banking, and digital finance, the nation's currency continues to trace its legitimacy to the Chikagoland Amethyst Crown.

## Modern Economy

Alamat operates as a modern fantasy society.

Citizens use Vyra through:

- Physical currency.
- Banking institutions.
- Digital payments.
- Salaries.
- Military payroll.
- Commercial trade.
- Everyday purchases.

The economy should feel familiar to modern players while existing naturally within the fantasy world.

## Symbolism

The inspiration behind Vyra comes from the Amethyst.

Within the world, the Amethyst represents:

- Wisdom.
- Humility.
- Compassion.
- Endurance.
- Hope.
- Leadership through service.

These values became permanently associated with the nation's identity after the survival of the Chikagoland Amethyst Crown during the Great Fire.

## Design Note

The name "Vyra" was intentionally chosen because it feels natural, modern, memorable, and timeless.

For players, Vyra simply becomes the currency of Alamat.

Behind the scenes, it quietly honors the Amethyst and the deeper themes of remembrance, resilience, and family that shape the world.

## Protected Canon

The official currency of Alamat is Vyra.

All future gameplay systems, shops, banking, military salaries, rewards, inventories, UI, and economic systems should reference Vyra as the canonical national currency.
