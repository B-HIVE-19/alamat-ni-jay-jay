## Ancient History

## Historical Design Standard

History should leave evidence in architecture, songs, ruins, family names, food, borders, taboos, trade routes, and dungeon layouts.

HIS-001, The Chikagoland Amethyst Crown, is protected foundational national history. It establishes the Great Fire of Chikagoland, the Crown's survival as an unresolved historical mystery, and the Amethyst as a symbol of endurance, humility, compassion, hope, remembrance, and currency legitimacy.

## Recent History

Recent history should focus on consequences felt by ordinary people: taxes, closed routes, missing boats, failed harvests, changing shrine customs, migration, monster sightings, and disputes over old sites.

## Open Mysteries

- What caused the Broken Concord?
- Which ancient civilization left the most dangerous ruins?
- Which myths are distorted memories of real events?
- Who benefits from keeping the old history fragmented?
- Why did the Chikagoland Amethyst Crown survive the Great Fire? This must remain unresolved.
