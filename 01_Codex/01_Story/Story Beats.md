## Purpose

This document tracks major story beats for *Alamat ni Jay Jay*.

No beat is approved until it supports character agency, emotional authenticity, the Alamat Doctrine, and established canon.

## Beat Template

| Field | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Beat ID | Unique identifier. |
| Title | Working or approved beat title. |
| Act / Chapter | Placement in structure. |
| Location | Where the beat occurs. |
| Characters Present | Who participates. |
| Character Choice | What choice drives the beat. |
| Conflict | What pressure or opposition exists. |
| Emotional Function | What the player should feel. |
| World Revelation | What the player learns about Alamat. |
| Foreshadowing | What is planted or paid off. |
| Consequence | What changes afterward. |
| Canon Status | Draft, Review, Approved, Deprecated. |
