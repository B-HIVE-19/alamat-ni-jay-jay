## Purpose

The Chapters Index tracks approved and draft story chapters for *Alamat ni Jay Jay*.

Chapters should be organized around emotional movement, player discovery, character choice, and memorable adventure moments rather than simple plot advancement.

## Chapter Standards

Each chapter should define:

- Purpose.
- Player emotion.
- Main location.
- Key characters.
- Central choice or pressure.
- Exploration hook.
- Dungeon or quest connection.
- Humor beat.
- Emotional memory.
- Foreshadowing.
- Ending state.
- Canon status.
