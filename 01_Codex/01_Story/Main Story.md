## Purpose

Main Story tracks the central narrative path of *Alamat ni Jay Jay*.

The main story should evolve from character choices, relationships, obligations, courage, mistakes, and discovery rather than relying on destiny alone.

## Main Story Standards

- Adventure comes first.
- Curiosity should reveal meaningful information, secrets, shortcuts, or emotional truth.
- Major story beats should create memories, not only move the plot.
- Jay Jay's journey must honor the tribute foundation without becoming biography.
- The party should feel missed when the game is over.
