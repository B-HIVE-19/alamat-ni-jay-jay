## Purpose

Dialogue Pass tracks the production review process for character voice, scene texture, humor, emotional truth, exposition control, and relationship evolution.

This is not a script. It is the quality bar used when reviewing scenes.

## Dialogue Pass Standards

Each reviewed scene should be checked for:

- Distinct character voice.
- Natural interruption, disagreement, teasing, silence, or comfort where appropriate.
- Information revealed through personality rather than lecture.
- Humor emerging from character and situation.
- Relationship movement.
- Emotional authenticity.
- No generic RPG exposition unless intentionally stylized and justified.

## Memory Test

Before approving important dialogue, ask:

Would the player remember how this person sounded without the name box?

Would 12-year-old Jeff want to hear what they say next?

Does this scene protect the heart of the project?
