## Purpose

This document tracks major character arcs across the story.

Character arcs should emerge from choices, relationships, fears, values, mistakes, humor, and growth. Combat utility is secondary.

## Arc Template

| Field | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Character | Character name. |
| Starting State | Who they are before major pressure. |
| Dream / Want | What they consciously want. |
| Fear | What they avoid or dread. |
| False Belief | What they believe that may not be true. |
| Pressure | What repeatedly challenges them. |
| Key Choices | Choices that reveal change. |
| Relationship Changes | How bonds evolve. |
| Emotional Turns | Major moments of growth, regression, or honesty. |
| Dungeon / Location Mirror | Place that reflects or challenges the arc. |
| End State | Who they become. |
| Canon Status | Draft, Review, Approved, Deprecated. |
