## Purpose

Side Stories tracks optional and supporting stories that deepen characters, villages, dungeons, creatures, legends, and the living world.

Side stories should reward curiosity and make the world feel like it continues beyond the main plot.

## Side Story Standards

- A side story should reveal something true about a person, place, tradition, fear, joke, or old wound.
- Rewards may include memory, trust, shortcuts, lore, music, visuals, items, or relationship changes.
- Avoid filler errands unless the errand reveals character or place.
- Side stories may be funny, eerie, tender, strange, practical, or tragic, but they must feel specific to Alamat.
