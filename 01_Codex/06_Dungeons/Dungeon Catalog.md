## Purpose

The Dungeon Catalog tracks all planned, draft, review, approved, deprecated, and archived dungeons.

No dungeon may be added to production solely because the game needs another combat space.

## Approval Requirements

Before a dungeon can move beyond Draft, it must define:

- Purpose.
- Legend.
- History.
- Construction.
- Architecture.
- Atmosphere.
- Lighting.
- Music direction.
- Environmental storytelling.
- Creature ecology.
- Puzzle design.
- Secret areas.
- Boss encounter.
- Treasure.
- Shortcuts.
- Map philosophy.
- Player emotions.
