## Purpose

Mythology tracks old stories that shape regions, routes, dungeons, rituals, ruins, and fears across Alamat.

Every major legend must contain truth. That truth may be historical, ecological, spiritual, political, emotional, or practical.

## Legend Design Philosophy

Legends should reward curiosity. A player who listens to elders, reads inscriptions, notices architecture, follows songs, or remembers jokes should uncover practical advantages and emotional meaning.

## Legend Categories

| Category | Function |
| --- | --- |
| Origin Legends | Explain islands, mountains, rivers, ruins, families, or customs. |
| Warning Legends | Protect people from routes, seasons, creatures, taboos, or political danger. |
| Dungeon Legends | Preserve why a dungeon exists and why people avoid it. |
| Creature Legends | Explain fear, coexistence, bargains, avoidance, or misunderstanding. |
| Family Legends | Carry grief, obligation, inheritance, courage, shame, or loyalty. |
| Political Legends | Justify authority, hide old crimes, or distort public memory. |
| Travel Legends | Encode tide, reef, weather, forest, mountain, or route knowledge. |
