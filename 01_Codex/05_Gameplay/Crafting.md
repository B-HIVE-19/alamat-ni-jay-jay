## Purpose

Crafting should connect resources, villages, artisans, exploration, repairs, upgrades, and memory.

Crafting must not become generic menu labor. It should make the world feel material, local, and lived-in.

## Crafting Principles

- Materials should come from believable geography, trade, weather, dungeons, creatures, or village economies.
- Recipes should feel learned from people, places, discoveries, or traditions.
- Crafting can support humor, family, friendship, and local identity.
- Upgrades should feel meaningful rather than endless numerical churn.

## Open Questions

- Which crafts exist in the first village?
- What material would make 12-year-old Jeff excited to find?
- Which crafted item carries emotional or family meaning?
