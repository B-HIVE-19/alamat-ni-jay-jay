## Design Intent

Magic should support classic JRPG readability while feeling native to the world, cultures, and folklore of *Alamat ni Jay Jay*.

Player-facing magic must align with the world's cultural, spiritual, and historical logic. See `../03_World/World Magic.md` for the world bible treatment of magic in society.
