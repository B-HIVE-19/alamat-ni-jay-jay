## Purpose

Party systems define how companions travel, fight, talk, disagree, support each other, and grow together.

The party should feel like people sharing an adventure, not interchangeable combat slots.

## Party Principles

- Characters are remembered before combat systems.
- Party mechanics should reflect relationships, trust, tension, humor, fear, and loyalty.
- Field behavior should make companions feel present during exploration.
- Combat synergy should come from character identity and relationship history.
- The player should miss these people after finishing the game.
