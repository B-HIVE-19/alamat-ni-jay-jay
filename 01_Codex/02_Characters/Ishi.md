## Core Read

Ishi is the party's weaponsmith and artisan — the forge is her language. She speaks through her craft. The forge is her world and her peace. She studies metal like others read books. She jokes. She teases. She pushes. But her loyalty is unbreakable.

Signature quotes:

> "Steel remembers the hands that shaped it."

> "Weapons can be replaced. People can't. That's why you fight — to protect what matters."

## Ishi's Forge — Weapons Store & Upgrade

Ishi runs the party's weapons store and upgrade services:

- Weapon Crafting
- Upgrades & Enhancements
- Repairs & Reinforcement
- Custom Engravings
- Unlock Hidden Forms
- Legendary Forging

Design note from sheet: every blade she makes carries purpose. Every upgrade tells a story. Every hero who leaves her forge becomes stronger — because of the work she's willing to do for them when no one is watching.
