## Purpose

Define the combat identity, weapon, magic style, and battlefield philosophy of Lexi and her lifelong companion, Pandi.

Lexi is not a warrior.

She is a merchant, caretaker, and steward of the House of Lexi.

She only enters battle when the people she serves are threatened.

Her combat style should always reflect who she is outside of battle.

## Role

Primary Role:
Support Mage

Secondary Role:
Battlefield Utility

Lexi strengthens others rather than seeking glory herself.

She protects.

She restores.

She creates opportunities.

She rarely delivers the finishing blow.

## Weapon

Primary Weapon:
An elegant handcrafted wand carved from ancient whitewood.

The wand is topped with a polished Amethyst crystal inspired by the Chikagoland Amethyst Crown.

The crystal softly glows whenever magic is used.

The wand represents wisdom, compassion, stewardship, and hope rather than destructive power.

It should feel like an heirloom passed down through generations of the House of Lexi.

## Magic Style

Lexi's magic is based on Wonder rather than destruction.

Examples include:

- Healing.
- Protective barriers.
- Magical shields.
- Enchantments.
- Status cleansing.
- Teleportation tricks.
- Illusions.
- Object manipulation.
- Temporary buffs.
- Magical merchant tools.
- Curiosity magic.

Her spells should feel elegant, whimsical, and beautiful.

Never brutal.

## Pandi

Species:
Winged Panda

Role:
Companion

Battle Partner

Best Friend

Pandi is never controlled separately.

Lexi and Pandi function as one battle unit.

## Pandi's Personality

Pandi is endlessly curious.

Free-spirited.

Gets distracted easily.

Loves children.

Loves shiny things.

Always trying to help.

Sometimes succeeds.

Sometimes accidentally creates chaos.

Despite this, when Lexi truly needs him, he always comes through.

## Combat Style

Pandi battles alongside Lexi in a style inspired by unconventional JRPG companion characters.

Pandi does not fight through strength.

He creates opportunities.

Examples:

- Distracts enemies.
- Delivers magical items.
- Interrupts attacks.
- Retrieves enchanted trinkets.
- Creates unexpected openings.
- Accidentally triggers bonus effects.
- Protects Lexi when she is vulnerable.

The battlefield should feel alive because of Pandi's unpredictable personality.

His actions should never feel random.

They should feel like a curious creature trying very hard to help.

## Visual Style

Lexi remains calm.

Precise.

Graceful.

Every spell appears practiced.

Pandi is energetic.

Animated.

Constantly moving.

Constantly reacting.

Together they create a beautiful contrast:

Order and Joyful Chaos.

## Character Rule

Players should never think:

"Lexi became a mage."

Players should think:

"Of course this is how Lexi fights."

Her combat style must always feel like a natural extension of the magical world she lives in every day.

## Final Doctrine

Lexi enters battle only when those she loves or the communities she serves are in danger.

Pandi follows because Lexi is his family.

Together they remind players that courage is not measured by strength alone.

Sometimes the greatest heroes are the ones who spend most of their lives helping others behind a counter.
