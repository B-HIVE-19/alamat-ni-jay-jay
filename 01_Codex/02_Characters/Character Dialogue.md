## Purpose

Character Dialogue tracks voice, speech patterns, humor, silence, emotional habits, and relationship-specific language for major characters.

This document complements `../01_Story/Dialogue/Dialogue Direction.md`.

## Dialogue Rule

Every major character should become recognizable from dialogue alone.

If the speaker name is hidden, the player should still infer who is speaking through rhythm, word choice, what the character notices, what they avoid, and how they handle fear, affection, humor, pride, and grief.
