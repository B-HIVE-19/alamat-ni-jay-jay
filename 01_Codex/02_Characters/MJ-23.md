## Core Read

The twenty-third iteration of Project MJ. An ancient magi-tech platform restored, refined, and bonded with Mark. More than a machine. A partner. A friend. Family.

Signature quote:

> "Ready when you are, Partner." — MJ-23

## Origin Notes (from sheet)

- Ancient magi-tech origin.
- Restored by Mark Bugay (sheet text "Mark Kahel" superseded by CAN-0013).
- 23rd iteration of Project MJ.
- Accidentally discovered synchronization capabilities.
- Inseparable partners from the start of the story (CAN-0008, CAN-0015).

## Hardware

- Primary Optic: expressive lens capable of advanced scanning, emotional display, and holographic projection.
- Stabilizer Vanes: adjust in real time for agile flight and precise maneuvering.
- Articulated Arms: deploy for interaction, repair, shields, analysis, and combat support.
- Amethyst Core: ancient energy source. Also houses memory crystals from previous MJ prototypes.
- Bee crest engraving: symbol of House Bernaldo legacy and protection (sheet text "Kahel Legacy" superseded by CAN-0013).

## Movement & Agility

Hover, Boost, Dash/Feint, Pivot Turn, Vertical Climb.

## Expressions

Neutral, Curious, Happy, Focused, Surprised, Annoyed — displayed through the primary optic lens.

## Battle Role (from battle UI mockup)

- Analyze: scans the battlefield and highlights threats, weak points, and optimal tactics.
- Support: shields, buffs, healing, barrier projection, and real-time assistance.
- Strike: coordinated attacks with pinpoint accuracy. No movement wasted.
- Synchronize: two minds. One will.

The battle HUD includes an MJ-23 panel with a SYNC percentage gauge.

## Overclock — Synchronization Mode

When Mark and MJ-23 achieve complete synchronization, their calculations, instincts, and movements become one. Time slows. Clarity becomes absolute.

> This is not rage. This is perfect focus.

Sequence:

1. Align — systems link; emotional and cognitive frequencies synchronize.
2. Unfold — vanes extend, arms deploy, core resonance increases.
3. Focus — all calculations become instantaneous; battlefield analysis complete.
4. Synchronize — one mind, one heartbeat, one will. Synchronization complete.

(The battle mockup renders this as: Focus → Align → Connect → Synchronize → Release.)

## Color Palette

Cream, gold, gray, purple (amethyst), blue.
