## Core Read

Kalika is the first companion in *Alamat ni Jay Jay*. She is the party's dreamer and observer — quiet, but she notices everything. Her heart holds dreams that she rarely says out loud, and she sees the magic in people.

Signature quote:

> "Some stories are meant to be lived… not just imagined."
