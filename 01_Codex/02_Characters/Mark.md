## Core Read

Mark is a brave explorer who travels alone, relying on his instincts, experience, and the values he carries from home. He doesn't explore for glory — he explores to protect what matters most.

Signature quote:

> "I don't just explore for myself. I explore so I can protect what matters most."

## Companion: MJ-23

Mark restored MJ-23, an ancient magi-tech companion platform, and is its inseparable bonded partner from the start of the story (CAN-0008, CAN-0015). They share the Overclock synchronization mode — Mark's signature ability (CAN-0009). See `MJ-23.md`.

His art sheet's "Companion: None (Independent)" and "travels alone" text is superseded; his self-reliant, independent streak survives as personality, not as literal solitude.

## Battle Style — Brave & Loyal

Mark relies on agility, quick thinking, and fearless determination. He protects his allies and turns any challenge into an opportunity.

Named panels on sheet: Scouts Ahead, Protects Friends, Finds Weakness, Unleashes Hope.
