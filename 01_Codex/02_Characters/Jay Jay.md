## Core Read

Jay Jay is the protagonist and emotional heart of *Alamat ni Jay Jay*. He is funny, awkward, loyal, brave, big-hearted, and optimistic. He may stumble and doubt himself, but when his friends are in danger he finds a courage he didn't know he had.

Signature quote:

> "I may not be the strongest… but I'll never leave my friends behind."

## Family

House Moralde (Eagle crest): Jay Jay, Kuya, and Kuya Ric (CAN-0003).
House Bernaldo (Bee crest): Nanay, Mark, and Lexi (CAN-0004). The two houses are clearly one extended family — surnames and house membership are distinct (CAN-0013, CAN-0014).

- Kuya (CHR-002): House Moralde. Shares the village workshop with Kuya Ric.
- Kuya Ric (Ricardo Rombaua, CAN-0014): House Moralde. Big brother of the party; teases him.
- Ishi (cousin): teases him relentlessly ("Hey Jay," "#2"), annoys him, supports him, believes in him.
- Mark (Mark Bugay, CAN-0013 — cousin): House Bernaldo; they share matching Heartstone Charms.
- Lexi (cousin): House Bernaldo.
- Nanay: House Bernaldo (CAN-0004); "Nanay's Kitchen" sits near Jay Jay's house in the village mockup — the heart of the home.
