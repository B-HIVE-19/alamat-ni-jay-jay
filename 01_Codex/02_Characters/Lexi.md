## Core Read

Lexi is the warm heart of every village she visits. Keeper of curiosities, stories, and little miracles. She fights not for glory, but to protect the people who feel like home.

House of Lexi motto:

> "Take Only What You Need. Leave With More Than You Came For."

## Joining Chapter (from storyboard sheet)

Lexi is not one of the founding four — she joins via a story chapter (CAN-0018): a "turn of events" village chapter where Lexi and Pandi join the team to protect what matters most. Theme: Wonder. Protection. Unpredictable Help.

Storyboard beats:

1. Lexi at her shop, tending customers and organizing magical trinkets. Pandi gets distracted by a butterfly.
2. Sudden danger: shadow creatures appear in the village, threatening the people.
3. Lexi: "We protect this village." Raises her wand. Pandi nods… kinda.
4. Pandi flutters off to get the "perfect" item… gets distracted (as always).
5. Pandi returns with the wrong item (a fish). Lexi facepalms softly.
6. Pandi keeps rummaging through his satchel… digging… digging… finally!
7. Pandi finds the right charm! Presents it like his greatest treasure.
8. Lexi uses the charm with her wand. A protective barrier forms. The village is safe.
9. Battle flow: Lexi casts support magic while Pandi disrupts, distracts, and creates openings.
10. Lexi heals and strengthens her friends. Pandi hands over potions and trinkets.
11. Combined finish: wonder magic overwhelms the shadows. The threat is defeated.
12. After battle: Lexi and Pandi return to what they love most — helping people.

Key notes from sheet:

- Lexi = Support, Protection, Utility, Wonder Magic.
- Pandi = Disrupt, Distract, Retrieve, Comic Relief, Loyal Heart.
- Amethyst Wand is an heirloom of the House of Lexi.
- Pandi's Satchel: always full of random things; always somehow has exactly what Lexi needs.
