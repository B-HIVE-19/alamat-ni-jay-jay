## Purpose

This document collects optional exploration conversations for the core cast of *Alamat ni Jay Jay*.

These conversations exist to make the player love the characters during travel, rest, danger, waiting, and discovery. They should reveal party chemistry without forcing exposition or turning banter into lore delivery.

## Canon Basis

This library follows `AC-111 Core Cast Dynamics`:

| Character | Core Function |
| --- | --- |
| Jay Jay | Emotional center |
| Kalika | Wisdom |
| Kuya Ric | Courage |
| Mark | Curiosity |

## Usage Rules

1. Avoid exposition.
2. Keep humor grounded in personality and trust.
3. Let affection, worry, and irritation coexist.
4. Do not introduce unapproved backstory, final biography, visual design, or gameplay mechanics.
5. Lines may be revised as individual character voices become more specific.
6. These conversations are optional exploration flavor unless later assigned to a specific scene.
