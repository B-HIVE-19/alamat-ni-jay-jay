## Purpose

Character Timeline tracks each major character's life before, during, and after the main story.

Characters should feel like people who existed before Jay Jay met them and would continue living if the main plot never began.

## Timeline Rules

- Do not invent locked backstory without approval.
- Track emotional history, obligations, relationships, routines, losses, fears, and private hopes.
- Combat growth should follow character growth, not replace it.
- Timeline entries should help writers, animators, combat designers, and artists understand how a person changes over time.
