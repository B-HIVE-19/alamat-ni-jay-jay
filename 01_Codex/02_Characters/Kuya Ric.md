## Core Read

Kuya Ric is the party's guardian, protector, and big brother. He acts tough and loud, but deep inside, he would move mountains for the people he calls family.

Signature quotes:

> "Tara. I'll go first."

> "Siga, family always."

## Personality

Loud, protective, has a huge heart, fiercely loyal. Always puts others first. Siga (tough-guy) energy on the outside, softness underneath.

Likes:

- Making people laugh
- Family
- Good food
- Old-school music

Dislikes:

- Cowards
- Bullies
- Seeing friends hurt

## Weapon: Bantay Cannon

A custom-built shoulder cannon with purple energy core. ("Bantay" — to guard/watch over.)

## Companion: Siga the Rottie

A Rottweiler in armored purple-and-gold barding.

- Fiercely loyal
- Protective
- Gentle with the family
- Intimidating to strangers
- Thinks he's a lap dog

Mood set: Alert, Happy, Playful, Gentle.

> "He doesn't just protect the family. He IS the family."

## Battle Style — Protect & Inspire

Kuya Ric draws enemy attention and shields his allies. He empowers the party with courage, support, and heavy firepower.

Abilities (examples):

- Guardian's Roar
- Shield Ally
- Cover Fire
- Iron Will
- Siga's Charge
- Unbreakable Bond

Named panels on sheet: Draws the Fire, Shield Wall, Guardian's Roar, Cover Fire, Unbreakable Bond.

## Workshop

The Village 01 mockup places "Kuya & Kuya Ric's Workshop" steps from the Great Balete in the starting village (Maravyne, CAN-0001) — "where things get fixed, improved, invented, and where Kuya Ric is always half in, half out." He shares the workshop with Kuya (CHR-002), a fellow House Moralde member.

Note: his sheet lists hometown as Baybay ng Alon. If the workshop is in Maravyne, he lives away from home — or the two names refer to the same place. See CR-009.
