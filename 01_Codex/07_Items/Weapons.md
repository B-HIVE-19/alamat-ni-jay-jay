## Purpose

Weapons in *Alamat ni Jay Jay* should feel like objects from the world, not abstract stat sticks.

Every major weapon type should connect to culture, materials, trade, craft, character personality, animation readability, and combat identity.

## Weapon Design Rules

- Personality comes before optimization.
- Weapons should support character expression.
- Materials should come from the world economy and natural resources.
- Visual silhouettes must be readable in battle.
- Upgrades should feel like discovery, craft, memory, or trust rather than simple number inflation.
- Legendary weapons must have legends with truth inside them.
