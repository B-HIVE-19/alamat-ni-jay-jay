## Design Intent

Equipment should create meaningful character identity, tactical choices, and world flavor without overwhelming the player with noise.

Equipment visuals are governed by `../10_Art Direction/Art Direction.md`. Weapons and armor should feel crafted, inherited, traded, repaired, blessed, improvised, or discovered rather than generic loot.
