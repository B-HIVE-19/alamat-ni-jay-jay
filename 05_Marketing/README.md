# 05_Marketing

Marketing and public-facing materials home for *Alamat ni Jay Jay*.

Use this folder for store-page drafts, public descriptions, key art planning, trailer notes, screenshots, press materials, community messaging, and launch communications.

Marketing material must follow the Project Charter, Alamat Doctrine, Art Bible, Music Bible, and approved canon.
