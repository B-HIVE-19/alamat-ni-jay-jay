## Purpose

This document preserves the permanent artistic standard for every future character in *Alamat ni Jay Jay*.

Artists are not creating new anime characters.

Artists are faithfully transforming real people into fantasy JRPG heroes while preserving emotional identity.

The highest priority is not beautiful artwork.

The highest priority is recognition.

Name:
