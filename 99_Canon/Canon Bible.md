## Purpose

The Canon Bible is the single source of truth for every approved aspect of *Alamat ni Jay Jay*.

This is not a design document.

This is not a brainstorming document.

It contains approved canon only.

If information is unknown, mark it as:

STATUS: TBD

Never invent missing lore.

## Final Governance Rule

The Alamat Codex is the highest authority for the project.

If artwork, writing, dialogue, gameplay, AI generations, future prompts, or marketing material ever conflict with the Canon Bible, the Canon Bible always wins.

Never overwrite canon.

Only append new discoveries after explicit approval from 1910 Creative Studios.
