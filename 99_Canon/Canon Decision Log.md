## CAN-0012

Name:
Chikagoland Amethyst Crown Foundational History v1.1

Description:
The Chikagoland Amethyst Crown is the oldest surviving symbol of the nation. The national currency Vyra was first minted under the authority of the Crown after the Great Fire of Chikagoland, and every Vyra still traces its legitimacy back to that authority. Purple symbolizes earned leadership, wisdom, compassion, humility, hope, endurance, and service rather than wealth.

Status:
LOCKED

Source:
`01_Codex/03_World/HIS-001 — The Chikagoland Amethyst Crown.md`; user-approved HIS-001 revision, June 28, 2026.

Last Updated:
June 28, 2026

Notes:
The Crown survived the Great Fire untouched. No definitive canonical explanation for its survival may be given. The Crown appears in national institutions and symbols, including the National Treasury, Vyra currency, government seals, military commissions, Air Force insignia, national museums, historic monuments, and official state ceremonies.
