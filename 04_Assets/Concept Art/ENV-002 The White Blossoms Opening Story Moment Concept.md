## Purpose

ENV-002 establishes the opening story moment concept for the morning gathering beneath the great balete tree when unexpected white blossoms appear.

This document is an environment concept and story-moment prompt. It does not finalize the playable scene, cinematic staging, village map, character costumes, dialogue, or final lore explanation for the blossoms.

## Naming Note

The prompt identifies the coastal village as Maravyne. Maravyne is treated here as the concept-facing village name pending World Bible, Village 01, and Creative Director approval. This document should not be read as final approval of the village's official name until the relevant world document is updated.

## Creative Intent

The scene should communicate awe, community, warmth, and belonging rather than mystery or danger.

The viewer should feel they have arrived at a place where everyday life is celebrated and wonder is shared.

## Master Environment Concept Prompt

Create a cinematic AAA JRPG environment concept painting depicting the opening morning gathering beneath the great balete tree in the coastal village of Maravyne.

It is early morning. Warm golden sunlight filters through the immense canopy of an ancient balete tree overlooking the sea.

Something extraordinary has happened.

The balete is covered in delicate white blossoms, even though it is not their season to bloom.

Rather than fear, the village responds with joy.

Handcrafted paper lanterns are being strung between the massive roots. Children laugh as they weave between the roots playing games. Villagers smile and gather beneath the tree, carrying baskets of food and fresh bread. Soft music fills the air.

Kalika, a young woman the same age as Jay Jay, stands on one of the great roots tying another lantern overhead. She is focused and quietly smiling, naturally leading the tradition without drawing attention to herself.

In the distance, the village elder stands at the base of the trunk with open arms, laughing as neighbors arrive.

Jay Jay has just entered the gathering. He looks upward for the first time, seeing the unexpected blossoms. His sketchbook hangs loosely in one hand as he pauses in quiet wonder before beginning to draw.

The atmosphere should communicate awe, community, warmth, and belonging rather than mystery or danger.

No visible magic. No glowing effects. No supernatural energy.

The beauty comes from the villagers' response to the unexpected, not from spectacle.

## Required Style

- AAA JRPG concept art.
- Painterly.
- Cinematic composition.
- Warm sunrise lighting.
- Rich environmental storytelling.
- Handcrafted architecture.
- Lush tropical vegetation.
- Highly detailed.
- Emotionally nostalgic.

## Composition Requirements

- The ancient balete tree should remain the primary environmental anchor.
- White blossoms should read as extraordinary but natural, delicate, and seasonal-disruptive rather than magical.
- Jay Jay's sketchbook should be visible enough to support the Sketchbook Test from ART-003.
- Kalika should appear naturally capable and quietly central without turning the composition into a character portrait.
- The village elder and arriving neighbors should make the gathering feel communal, not ceremonial spectacle.
- Lanterns, food baskets, bread, children, music, roots, sunlight, sea air, and handcrafted village details should create the feeling of a shared tradition.

## Negative Direction

Do not include:

- Visible magic.
- Glowing effects.
- Supernatural energy.
- Fearful villagers.
- Monster, war, soldier, or threat imagery.
- Dark omen framing.
- Overly sacred spectacle that makes the moment feel distant from everyday life.
- Final character costume details that would override Character Lock approval.
