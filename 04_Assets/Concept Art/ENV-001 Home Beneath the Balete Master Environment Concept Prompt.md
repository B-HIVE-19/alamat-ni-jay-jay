## Purpose

ENV-001 establishes the master environment concept prompt for the opening village emotional center: a coastal home space gathered beneath an old balete tree.

This document is an art production prompt, not a final lore bible, map, asset sheet, or approved playable layout. It should guide concept art generation and environment art exploration while remaining subordinate to the World Bible, Village design standards, ART-002, and future approved location documents.

## Creative Intent

The image should communicate the emotional thesis of home before adventure begins.

The immediate viewer response should be:

"I'd like to live here."

## Master Environment Concept Prompt

Create a highly detailed AAA JRPG environment concept illustration for an original fantasy world inspired by Filipino values, not historical Philippines.

The scene depicts the emotional heart of a small coastal village at sunrise. At the center stands a magnificent centuries-old balete tree with an enormous canopy providing shade over a stone gathering plaza. The tree is the emotional center of village life, not because it possesses magic, but because generations have gathered beneath it.

The village overlooks a calm turquoise sea with gentle waves, small wooden fishing boats, distant tropical islands, and lush green mountains disappearing into the morning mist.

Warm golden sunrise light filters through the branches, creating soft rays and dappled shadows across handcrafted stone paths.

Children laugh and play around the tree while elders sit together telling stories. Fishermen prepare their boats along a small dock. Neighbors greet one another by name. Smoke rises from kitchens preparing breakfast. Chickens wander between homes. Laundry gently sways in the ocean breeze. Every person appears to know everyone else.

The architecture is handcrafted using tropical hardwoods, woven bamboo, carved stone, and nipa-inspired roofing interpreted through an original fantasy aesthetic. Avoid historical reconstruction. This is an original world inspired by Filipino values of family, hospitality, community, humility, and shared responsibility.

The village should feel intimate, peaceful, walkable, and deeply lived in. Every building should appear unique, occupied, and loved. The environment should communicate that people have lived here for generations.

There are no castles, no visible soldiers, no war, no monsters, no ruins, and no visible magic.

The emotional focus is home, belonging, kindness, and everyday life before adventure begins.

The artwork should evoke the nostalgic emotional feeling of classic JRPG villages while achieving modern AAA environment quality. Painterly concept art with realistic lighting, handcrafted textures, lush tropical vegetation, atmospheric perspective, cinematic composition, and extraordinary environmental storytelling.

## Required Color Palette

- Warm sunrise gold.
- Tropical greens.
- Turquoise ocean.
- Weathered natural wood.
- Warm earth tones.
- Natural stone.
- Soft blue sky.

## Required Mood

- Safe.
- Warm.
- Welcoming.
- Hopeful.
- Peaceful.
- Alive.

## Composition Requirements

- Aspect ratio: 16:9.
- High-resolution AAA environment concept art.
- The balete tree and stone gathering plaza should be the central emotional anchor.
- Sunrise light should visibly connect the village, sea, homes, paths, and people.
- The sea, boats, islands, mountains, and village life should support the feeling of a lived-in coastal community rather than compete with the central tree.

## Negative Direction

Do not include:

- Castles.
- Visible soldiers.
- War imagery.
- Monsters.
- Ruins.
- Visible magic.
- Historical reconstruction.
- Generic medieval fantasy architecture.
- Empty stage-set buildings.
- Overly grand spectacle that weakens the intimacy of home.
